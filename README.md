# Workshop 1

Five simple programs:
1. Guessing numbers - computer chooses random number from 1 to 100 and player is guessing.
2. Lottery simulator - computer randomly chooses 6 numbers from 1 to 49, player chooses his set. There is a prize for 3, 4, 5 and 6 hits.
3. Guessing numbers 2 - now player thinks of a number and computer is trying to guess it.
4. Dice roll - program reads encoded string, decodes it an rolls a dice.
5. Most popular words - program finds most popular words from news websites and counts them.
